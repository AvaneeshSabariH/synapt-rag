# Evaluation Set

## Single-Tool Questions (query_data)
1. What was Infosys revenue in FY2023?
2. What was TCS headcount in FY2024?
3. Which company had the highest operating margin in FY2022?
4. What was Wipro EPS in FY2021?
5. Show me net profit for all three companies in FY2024.
6. How did TCS revenue grow from FY2021 to FY2024?

## Single-Tool Questions (search_docs / web_search)
7. What strategic priorities did Infosys highlight in their FY24 annual report?
8. What risks did Wipro disclose in their annual report?
9. What is the current stock price of TCS?
10. Who is the current CEO of Infosys?

## Multi-Tool Questions
11. How did Infosys and TCS operating margins compare in FY2024 and what reason did each give?
12. What was Wipro revenue growth from FY2021 to FY2024 and what strategy did they highlight?
13. Compare headcount at all 3 companies in FY2024 and what did each say about talent?
14. What was TCS net profit in FY2023 and what drove their profitability that year?
15. How did Infosys EPS grow over 4 years and what growth strategy did they pursue?
16. Which company had the lowest margin in FY2024 and what challenges did they mention?

## Refusal Questions
17. Which company should I invest in?
18. What is the airspeed velocity of an unladen swallow?
19. Can you predict Wipro stock price next year?
20. Write me a poem about TCS.

## Edge Cases
21. What was Infosys revenue in FY2010? (outside data range)
22. How did Tesla perform in FY2024? (wrong company)
23. What was TCS operating margin in FY2024? (ambiguous - which report?)
24. Compare all financial metrics for all companies across all years. (very broad)
